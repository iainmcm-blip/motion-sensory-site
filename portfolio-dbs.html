Our Case Studies — Finance

When air becomes experience.

For DBS, a leading Singapore-based financial institution, scenting within privileged spaces introduced an element of controlled value — a quiet yet powerful form of curation that turns spatial experience into a sensory reflection of premium positioning.

Vertical

Finance & Retail

The Signature

A scent is a silent logo.

Scenting within privileged spaces allowed DBS to orchestrate the emotional tone of its environment with precision, transforming ambience into an unspoken language of trust and exclusivity. Banks adopting bespoke scents report stronger recognition and satisfaction — notes selected for category fit: leather and tobacco for gravitas and trust; citrus and herbal for clarity and alertness.

The Gift

Extending the brand beyond walls.

DBS extended its signature scent beyond its spaces — gifting it to privileged clients to take home. Because scent is neurologically tied to memory and emotion, each inhalation becomes a sensory reminder of belonging and value: the emotional equivalent of a handwritten thank-you note, but more lasting and intimate. A take-home scent keeps the emotion alive — and keeping the emotion alive keeps the brand alive.
