Our Case Studies — Singapore Airshow

A signature scent experience at Asia’s premier aviation stage.

For the 10th edition of the Singapore Airshow, we introduced a layered scenting strategy that elevated the visitor journey through one of the region’s largest aviation exhibitions — reinforcing brand identity for one of aviation’s most recognised institutions.

Aviation & Events

The Fragrance

Introducing Timely.

We developed Timely — a bespoke environmental fragrance built on notes of green leaves, aquatic and cedarwood, designed to mirror the clarity, dynamism and forward momentum of the aviation industry. It was paired with the iconic airport scent developed for Changi Airport Group, scaling a recognisable identity across a vast event environment.

The Engineering

Precision across a vast showfloor.

Diffused across an expansive environment with soaring, dome-shaped ceilings, our strategy required a standalone deployment in place of conventional HVAC integration. Through a carefully engineered network of individual diffusion units — approximately three times the typical system density — we achieved a consistent, memorable atmospheric presence across the entire showfloor.
