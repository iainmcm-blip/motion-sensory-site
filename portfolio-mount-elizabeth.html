Our Case Studies — Medical

Where care becomes luxury.

Parkway Pantai’s Mount Elizabeth Novena Hospital reimagines the very experience of care — a fusion of hospital and hotel where ambient scent transforms functional space into an expression of five-star hospitality across all 13 levels.

Vertical

Medical & Wellness

The Concept

A five-star welcome.

From architectural detailing and interior furnishings to staff attire and curated patient meals, every private level reflects the refinement of a five-star hotel. A signature ambient scent installed across all 13 levels — including medical suites — reframes cost as investment, transforming clinical environments into expressions of luxury care, free of the unwelcoming odours hospitals are notorious for.

The Emergency Touchpoint

Scent as emotional anchoring.

When people arrive in emergency, their bodies are often in a heightened fight-or-flight state — elevated heart rate, cortisol and adrenaline. A scent diffused with notes of cleanliness and light medicinal freshness subconsciously signals order, control and safety. Certain aromatic molecules are proven to reduce sympathetic nervous activity, easing tension and restoring calm. Here, scent is used not as decoration, but as communication: ‘You can exhale now.’
