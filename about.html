About Us

The minds behind the senses.

Founded in Singapore, Motion Sensory is a collective of strategists, perfumers, sonic designers and sensory specialists — united by a belief that every space should feel like something deliberate.

Juliana Mattar

Founder & Chief Sensory Officer

With over 26 years in multisensory design and experiential innovation, Juliana is an early advocate for sensory thinking in brand strategy — bridging creativity, psychology and cultural insight. Grounded in stagecraft, media and storytelling, she has grown Motion from a Singapore studio into Asia Pacific and the Middle East, uniting creativity with culture and emotion with behaviour.

Aaliyah Hamdoon

Creative Director · Founder, LA Scenting

Aromachologist and founder of LA Scenting. Aaliyah believes the power of scenting lies in immersion — the ability to transport across time and space. As Official Scent Designer for Changi Singapore's award-winning airport, she scents environments from corporate to retail, evoking emotion and encoding the unique perception of a brand.
