Spatial Design · Immersive Storytelling

Aesop: Khronos

An experience of unhurried skincare set within the verdant surroundings of Fort Canning — reinforcing the synergy between skin, environment and mindful care.

Location

Fort Canning Heritage Gallery, Singapore

01 / The Challenge

Architectural Silence

Aesop sought to create a transient physical manifestation of their brand philosophy. We translated brand continuity into an immersive, spatial form, creating a living metaphor for skin as a surface that responds and breathes. The single-surface architectural language materiality echoes the skin's porous texture.

"The experience was engineered not just for beauty, but for cultural impact, media resonance, and long-term brand equity."

02 / The Sensory Approach

15,000 tubes of intention

Over 15,000 hand-placed cardboard tubes enveloped guests in a single-surface architectural installation. By blurring interior and exterior, we embedded the experience within its natural context, aligning skincare with environment and mindfulness.
